Time Table Management

Employees of any organization, for work purposes, need to communicate with their interdepartmental counterparts regularly. This procedure is usually tedious and time-consuming as it needs to go through various levels of corporate hierarchy. Often, the officials have to schedule meetings such that most of the employees working for him can attend it. Each employee may not be idle at the moment, making it difficult for them to determine the most appropriate time for the official gathering. In this project, I am trying to address the above-mentioned problem by making a web application for timetable management that can be useful for each staff of organizations pursuing fixed time schedules. The web application would include all workers' schedules that can be updated by them in real time. The application would display the idle time slots, as per the user request. The meetings can be scheduled on the approval of most of the participating individuals. The application would speed up the aforementioned process and make it simpler.
